Mission Statement

We love music!

Our mission is to help people experience abundant life in Christ. The heartbeat of our church is to create an authentic worship environment where people can encounter God, experience life-giving community and be empowered to fulfill their purpose. We believe that life is better together and we hope that you will come and connect with us.


TEACHER SCHOOL SUPPLY DRIVE

Sunday, July 24th & Sunday, July 31st

Help us bless the teachers at Abney Elementary School with the needed supplies for a successful school year! Supplies can be dropped off on Sundays in the dropbox next to the connection table. Below is a list of needed supplies:

  • pens
  • pencils
  • dry erase markers
  • bright colored printer paper
  • mounting tape / 3M command strips
  • kleenex
  • hand sanitizer
  • amazon gift cards

5TH SUNDAY

SUNDAY, JULY 31ST // AFTER SERVICE

Join us for communion, water baptisms, baby dedications, food & fellowship on the last Sunday of the month. There will also be a slip n' slide and Kona Ice after service so bring a change of clothes to join in on the fun! If you're interested in water baptism or baby dedication, please sign up at the connection table on Sunday, July 24th.
